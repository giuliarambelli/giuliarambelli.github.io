I am a PhD candidate in Linguistic studies at the University of Pisa in cotutelle with the Aix-Marseille University (AMU). I am a member of the {{< staticref "http://colinglab.humnet.unipi.it/" "newtab" >}}Computational Liguistics Laboratory  {{< /staticref >}} (University of Pisa) and of the {{< staticref "http://www.lpl-aix.fr/" "newtab" >}}Laboratoire Parole et Language {{< /staticref >}}  (AMU). I am also enrolled in the  {{< staticref "https://www.ilcb.fr/" "newtab" >}}Institute of Language, Communication, and the Brain {{< /staticref >}}(ILCB) PhD Program.


My research field are Lexical and Computational Semantics, and Natural Language Processing. In my research, I investigate the mechanisms underlying natural language comprehension, bringing together the theoretical framework of Construction Grammars, usage-based models as Distributional Semantics and psycholinguistic findings.
