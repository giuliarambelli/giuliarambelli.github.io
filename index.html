Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Linguistic studies at the University of Pisa in cotutelle with the Aix-Marseille University (AMU), under the joint supervision of Alessandro Lenci and Philippe Blache. I am a member of the Computational Liguistics Laboratory (University of Pisa) and of the Laboratoire Parole et Language (AMU). I am also enrolled in the Institute of Language, Communication, and the Brain (ILCB) PhD Program.

My research field are Lexical and Computational Semantics, and Natural Language Processing. In my research, I investigate the mechanisms underlying natural language comprehension, bringing together the theoretical framework of Construction Grammars, usage-based models as Distributional Semantics and psycholinguistic findings.

Interests

  • Computational Semantics
  • Usage-based models of language
  • Experimental methods for the study of language
